About Me

A story of good

Ciao! I'm Andrea Ferrari, an Italian with a passion for both technology and the environment. For many years, I've been living abroad, embracing new cultures and experiences. Currently, I'm immersed in the world of full-stack web development as a student at Start 2 Impact University. My journey has been anything but ordinary, having previously worked as a scuba diving instructor, photographer, and environmental filmmaker. I've explored the depths of the oceans and captured their beauty through my camera lens while advocating for sustainable development. My unique background has shaped my commitment to creating innovative and sustainable solutions, both in the digital realm and for our planet. In a world where coding and creativity collide, I'm on a mission to make a positive impact.
